About

Currently I am a visiting student at UCLA, and I just graduated from Peking University this summer. I'm honored to be supervised by Xiang 'Anthony' Chen (UCLA), Pu Yan (Peking University) and Xiaoguang Wang (Wuhan University). My research focuses on human-centered AI. I am particularly interested in understanding how users interact with generative AI systems and how their prompt literacy can be supported through cognitive and behavioral signals.
