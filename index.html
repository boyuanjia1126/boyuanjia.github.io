About

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA). My research focuses on AI literacy, eye-tracking, and human-centered AI. I am particularly interested in understanding how users interact with generative AI systems and how their prompt literacy can be supported through cognitive and behavioral signals.

Research & Projects

Prompt Literacy and Eye-Tracking Study
Investigating how users’ eye-movement patterns and think-aloud data reveal their cognitive processes when interacting with AIGC tools.

AI Literacy Framework Development
Building a multidimensional framework for AI literacy among young adults, integrating survey, behavioral, and interview data.
